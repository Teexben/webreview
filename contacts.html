Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy? +

We offer flexible cancellation policies. Cancellations made 7 days or more before check-in receive a full refund. Between 3-7 days before check-in, we charge 50% of the total booking amount. Cancellations within 72 hours of check-in are non-refundable.

Do you offer airport transfers? +

Yes, we provide airport transfer services from Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO) at an additional charge. Please inform us of your flight details at least 48 hours in advance. Our driver will meet you at the arrivals hall with a signboard.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, bank transfers, and cash (USD, EUR, TZS). A 30% deposit is required to confirm your reservation, with the balance due upon arrival.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? +

Absolutely. Our restaurant can accommodate various dietary needs including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and halal options. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when making your reservation.
