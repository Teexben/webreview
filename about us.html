Our Story

Discover the heritage behind Boma Villa Kilimanjaro

A Legacy of Hospitality

Founded in 2010 by the Mushi family, Boma Villa Kilimanjaro began as a vision to create a sanctuary where travelers could experience authentic Tanzanian hospitality amidst the breathtaking beauty of Mount Kilimanjaro.

"Boma" in Swahili means a homestead or enclosure, reflecting our commitment to creating a warm, welcoming environment where every guest feels at home. Over the years, we've grown from a small family guesthouse to a premier luxury villa, all while maintaining our core values of hospitality, sustainability, and cultural preservation.
